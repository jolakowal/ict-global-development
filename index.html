About the Workshop

Welcome to the ICT and Global Development (SIG GLOBDEV) Workshop titled "Bridging Horizons: Empowering Global Development through ICT and Social Entrepreneurship". This workshop aims to explore the intersection of information and communication technologies (ICT) and social entrepreneurship to foster global development.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to submit their papers on topics related to ICT and social entrepreneurship for global development. Selected papers will be presented at the workshop.
